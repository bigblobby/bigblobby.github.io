Tom Dempster

Front-end Developer

My journey began on November 27th 2017, Cyber Monday funnily enough. I was trawling through the internet looking for my next venture, then Web Development cropped up. Thats when I decided, this is what I want to do. I've always had an interest in computers and tech since as long as I can remember. I still can't believe its taken me this long to find programming, but I've found it and so it begins.

I currently enjoy building things from scratch(no, not the language) as I like the challenge of creating new designs and flexibility a blank canvas provides me, this isn't a viable option for every project though, which is why WordPress and other Content Managment Systems are on my radar.

I have a special interest in minimalism, UX, and animation, and I believe websites not only need to look good, but they need to be easy to navigate and accessible to everyone.
